## Risk Register Governance Note
The **security analyst maintains the register**, James Chen owns overall quality and escalation, and Sarah Park updates technical treatment status for infrastructure risks. The register should be reviewed **monthly** at the security governance meeting and **quarterly** with executive leadership. Out-of-cycle review is triggered by any major incident, KEV-listed exposure on a critical asset, failed restore test, material audit finding, or a KRI threshold breach. When a KRI is breached, the risk owner must update the entry within five business days, propose corrective action, and escalate any change from Medium to High/Critical residual risk to James Chen and the CEO.
